Is the claim against an organisation or a person?

For example, if the dispute is about a holiday pay, the claim should be made against the organisation named in the contract or letter of employement not the HR or the line manager.
To avoid jeopardising your case we will need the legal name of the organisation. For example, PRET A MANGER LIMITED. Please get the name from a P45, P60, payslip, contract or letter of employment. If you do not have one of these documents, you can look to find the organisation's address on Companies House search on GOV.UK.
To avoid jeopardising your case we will need their legal address. Please get the address from your a P45, P60, payslip, contract or letter of employment. If you do not have one of these documents, you can look to find the organisation's address on Companies House search on GOV.UK.
If you work or worked with this person, you may want to give the organisation's address. Please get the name from a P45, P60, payslip, contract or letter of employment. If you do not have one of these documents, you can look to find the employers address on Companies House search on GOV.UK.
