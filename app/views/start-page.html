Find a solution to your employment dispute

Use this service to start the legal process in which you notify Acas that you want to:

  • try our free early conciliation service
  • get a certificate to make a claim to an employment tribunal

If you are unable to use this online service, please call us on 0300 123 1122.

Start now

Before you start

To use this service, you’ll need:

  • identity of your employer like comapny name and address the correct legal name and address of the organisation or employer - find this on a recent letter, payslip, P60 or P45
  • your representation details like name and address

Help applying online

Call Acas early conciliation team

Telephone: 0300 123 1122
Monday to Friday, 8am to 6pm

Calls cost 12p a minute from a landline, and from 3p to 45p a minute from a mobile.

Make sure you keep a note of the date you called and the name of the person you spoke to. They’ll fill in the form for you over the phone.
