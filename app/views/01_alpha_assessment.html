Service descirption

Find a solution to your employment dispute

Use this service to start the legal process in which you tell Acas that:

  • you have a dispute with your employer
  • you intend to make a claim to an employment tribunal

Service users

Claimant and Conciliator.
  1. As a claimant I need to understand what information I need to provide so that a concialiator can effectively handle my case
  2. As a conciliator I need parties to provide correct legal and representation details (e.g. legal identity of employer, own details, representation details), so that I can avoid jeopardising their case, progress the case swiftly and reduce the risk of running out of time

2. Solve a whole problem for users

How the service intends to solve one whole problem for users, collaborating across organisational boundaries where necessary.

  • Problem to be solved

    Offer our users the simplest and most accessible journey to help them resolve their employment dispute at the earliest point possible, and ensure that they experience a high-quality, value-added service that leads to an appropriate outcome for their case.

    Explore targeted microguidance and other notification form interventions that will help users notify successfully, reduce invalid or out of time cases and improve the quality of the information provided to conciliators to help them resolve cases faster and more often.
