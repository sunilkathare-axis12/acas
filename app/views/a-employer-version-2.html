Is the claim against an organisation or a person?

For example, if the dispute is about a holiday pay, the claim should be made against the organisation named in the contract or letter of employement not the HR or the line manager.
To avoid jeopardising your case we will need the legal name of the organisation. For exmaple, PRET A MANGER LIMITED. Please copy the name from your P45, P60, payslip, contract or letter of employment.
To avoid jeopardising your case we will need their legal address. This may be the head office or registered office address and not your workplace. Please copy the address from your P45, P60, payslip, contract or letter of employment.
To avoid jeopardising your case we will need the correct full legal name of the person. For exmaple, Joe Bloggs.
To avoid jeopardising your case we will need to give us their legal address. If you work or worked with this person, you may want to give the organisation's address. This may be the head office or registered office address and not your workplace. Please copy the address from your P45, P60, payslip, contract or letter of employment. If you can find the orgainsation on GOV.UK companies house register, you can copy and paste the registered address from here
